The following are the rules for the Grand Olympiad.
Grand Olympiad Match takes place every happens day from 20:00 to 00:00.
Only matches without class distinction and class-based games will take place.
The Winner is determined by how much damage given. If damage is inflicted to the Summoner's Servitor, Maestro's Golem or Hell Knight's Dark Panther, it does not have any influence on the scores in the game.
